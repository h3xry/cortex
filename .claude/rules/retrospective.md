# Retrospective (rrr)

## Output Files
- `.claude/memory/retrospective/YYYY-MM-DD/N-HHMM-description.md`
- `.claude/memory/lesson/YYYY-MM-DD.md`

## Required Sections
- [ ] Timeline with specific times
- [ ] What Went Well (specific)
- [ ] What Didn't Go Well (root cause)
- [ ] Key Learnings (actionable)
- [ ] Action Items (measurable)
- [ ] **AI Diary (mandatory)**

## AI Diary Questions
- How was working with user today?
- How did I feel about feedback received?
- What did I do poorly?
- What am I proud of?
- What do I want to improve?
- Honest feedback to user (if any)
